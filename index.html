Frequently asked questions

How does pricing work for Business teams?

Business is $4.99/month per team member, plus usage-based pricing for Relationship Intelligence features like Lead Capture and AI Notetaker. You get 500 credits included annually (~$500 value).

What are credits and how do they work?

Credits are how you pay for Relationship Intelligence features. Scanning a contact costs 5 credits ($5), exporting to your CRM costs 5 credits ($5), and AI Notetaker costs $0.25 per minute. Business plans include 500 free credits annually. If you need more, additional credits are $1 each.

What's included for free?

Contact enrichment is always free. We automatically add LinkedIn profiles, company data, and more to every contact you capture. Connecting your CRM and adding notes and tags are also free.

What CRMs does Blinq integrate with?

Blinq natively integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, and Outlook. We also connect to 5,000+ apps through Zapier.

What's the difference between Pro and Business?

Pro is for individual power networkers who want unlimited scanning and AI Notetaker. Business is for teams who need admin controls, CRM sync, and centralized contact management.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes. Free users get 5 scans and 300 minutes of AI Notetaker to try. Business teams can start a free trial with 500 credits included.
