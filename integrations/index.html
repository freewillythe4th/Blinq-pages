Frequently asked questions

How do I create a digital business card?

Creating your Blinq digital business card takes less than a minute. Start by creating your card, or by downloading the app (iOS/Android). You'll be guided through the card creation process, where you can add your contact details, a profile photo, and customize your card with your company logo. Once done, you'll be ready to start sharing straight away.

Is Blinq free to use?

Yes! Blinq is free for everyone. You’ll get access to digital business cards, virtual backgrounds, and dynamic email signatures. With Blinq Premium, you unlock added customization and export options, which are free to trial for 7 days.

How do I share my Blinq card with someone who doesn't have the app?

If the recipient doesn’t have the Blinq app, your card will open in their web browser. They can easily save your details and share theirs back with you, making it easy and convenient to reconnect.

How do I share a digital business card?

Sharing your digital business card is super simple. You can have recipients scan the QR code from your Blinq app, or share it via text, email, Widgets, Apple Wallet, or even from your Apple Watch. It’s instant and convenient—perfect for sharing in person or online.

What’s the difference between a digital business card and a traditional business card?

A Blinq digital business card is like a traditional business card, but made for modern networking! No more printing costs, cards getting lost, or outdated details. It’s always on your phone, ready to share, and  can capture the details of the person you're meeting making it easy to follow-up—plus, it’s eco-friendly and completely customizable to match your business.

‍

What are the benefits of using a digital business card?

Share your contact details easily: A digital business card is the quickest and easiest way to share your professional identity.
‍
‍Your card is always with you: Whether you prefer to share using the app, from your Apple Wallet, your smart watch or home screen widgets, it will be with you wherever you and your device goes, no need to carry paper cards anymore.
‍
‍Works every time: Those who receive your card don’t need the Blinq app to get your contact details.
‍
‍Always stay up-to-date: If you change your contact information on your QR code business card, those who have your card will see your updated information.

‍

How do I get Blinq for my team or business?

Setting up Blinq for your team is easy. With Blinq Business, you can manage multiple cards, customize them with your branding, and sync your team's contacts directly with your CRM. Check out our Blinq Business subscription for more details.

‍

How much does a digital business card cost?

You can create your own Blinq digital business card for free! Start by downloading our top rated iOS app or Android app, or get started using our web dashboard.
‍
Our onboarding process will guide you through the setup process including how to customize your card with logos, profile images, colours and more. You’ll then be ready to share your digital business card in seconds!
‍
You can also sign up for Blinq Premium to create more cards for yourself or for Blinq Business if you’re looking to create cards for your wider business team. For full pricing information view our Pricing Page.

‍

How do NFC business cards work?

Blinq offers NFC or ‘Near Fields Communication’ business cards for you and your team. NFC cards have a chip embedded in each card that can be synced with your Blinq digital business card.
‍
When you want to share your details, simply tap the card on the user's compatible smartphone which will bring up a link containing your Blinq card, it really is like magic. You can purchase NFC cards via our app or our website today. Alternatively, if you already have an NFC card made by another company, you can encode it with your Blinq card by encoding your Blinq card link onto it.

How does Blinq integrate with my existing tech stack?

Blinq Business has several integrations with tools your team already uses, like HubSpot, Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and more. An example of how this plays out for teams: you can sync your contacts as leads directly into your CRM, automatically provision your team's cards using Microsoft Entra, and remotely manage dynamic email signatures using Google Workspace.

What are dynamic email signatures, and how do I create one with Blinq?

Dynamic email signatures let you extend your brand with every email you send. With Blinq, you can create professional, branded signatures that match your digital business card in just a few clicks.

What are virtual backgrounds, and how can I use them with Blinq?

Blinq’s virtual backgrounds let you add a professional touch to your video calls with a branded QR code embedded directly into your custom background. Simply choose one of our backgrounds or upload your own and showcase your brand during virtual meetings.
